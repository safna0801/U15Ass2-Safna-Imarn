Dates&Fees

2024/2025 Academic Year Dates & Fees

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we divide our academic year into four terms: Autumn, Winter, Spring, and Summer. This structure allows us to design learning themes and activities that align with both seasonal and cultural events, while also ensuring alignment with South Manchester (Trafford Council) school holidays.

We offer Saturday Slovak Language Lessons at two convenient locations:

  • Morning Sessions (AM): 10:00 – 12:00 at The Firs Primary School
  • Afternoon Sessions (PM): 14:00 – 16:00 at Sale High School

While we primarily serve South Manchester, we welcome students from all areas.

2024/2025 Term Dates

Term Start Date End Date Weeks Holidays
Autumn 2nd November 2024 14th December 2024 7 weeks Christmas holiday after term ends
Winter 11th January 2025 8th February 2025 5 weeks February holiday after term ends
Spring 1st March 2025 17th May 2025 10 weeks Easter holiday (no classes on 12th & 19th April), May holiday after term ends

Note: Term dates align with Trafford Council school holidays where possible, though some variations may apply.
Fees and Payment Options

We offer two flexible payment options to cater to different family needs:

Term Fee
Autumn Term (7 weeks) £84
Winter Term (5 weeks) £60
Spring Term (10 weeks) £120
Summer Term (6 weeks) £72

Sibling Discount: Each additional sibling receives a 25% discount on their term fees.

Payment Options:

  • Term Fees: For committed learners who wish to pay upfront for the entire term.
  • Pay-As-You-Go: For families who prefer the flexibility of paying on a week-to-week basis.
