Slovak School Registration

All students enrolling in our Slovak School must complete a registration form to ensure we provide the best possible learning experience tailored to their needs.

How to Register

  1. Complete the School Registration Form (Online form link).
  2. Submit your form:
    • Online: Fill out and submit electronically.
    • In-person: Bring the completed form to your first session.
    • By email: Scan and send your completed form to your@email.com.
  3. Receive confirmation via email with further details about your first session.

Required Information

  • Student details (name, age, language background)
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Medical or special educational needs (SEN) information
  • Preferred session (AM or PM)
  • Consent for photos, videos, and data handling

Volunteer Registration

We welcome enthusiastic volunteers who want to support our school and cultural activities.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. Complete the Volunteer Registration Form (Online form link).
  2. Submit your form:
    • Online: Submit electronically.
    • In-person: Hand it in during a session.
    • By email: Send it to svcmanchester@yahoo.com.
  3. DBS Check (if applicable) – Volunteers working directly with children may require a DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check. We can guide you through the process if needed.
  4. Attend an introductory meeting to discuss your role and contribution.

Volunteer Roles Available

  • Teaching Assistant – Support our educators in lessons.
  • Event Coordinator – Help plan and organize cultural events.
  • Fundraising & Sponsorship – Assist with grant applications and funding initiatives.
  • Social Media & Marketing – Help promote our activities and share updates.
  • General Support – Assist with setup, administration, and community engagement.

Monthly Membership Interest Form

We are developing a monthly membership program for families who want extra resources, preparation for "komisionálne skúšky," and additional learning support.

Who is this for?

  • Families whose children are registered in school in Slovakia and need support preparing for exams.
  • Students looking for extra Slovak language resources beyond weekly classes.
  • Those interested in additional cultural and educational activities.

If you would like to become a member, please fill out our Monthly Membership Interest Form (Online form link). We will contact you once the program is finalised.

Consent Form

To ensure the safety, privacy, and well-being of all students, we require a completed Consent Form for everyone enrolled at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester.

What the Consent Form Covers

  • Emergency Medical Treatment
  • Photo & Video Consent (Optional)
  • Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)
  • Participation in Off-Site Activities & School Trips (Optional)
  • Special Educational Needs (SEN) & Medical Information

How to Submit the Consent Form

  1. Complete the form here (Online form link).
  2. Submit the form:
    • Online: Fill out and submit electronically.
    • In-person: Bring the completed form to your first session.
    • By email: Scan and send it to svcmanchester@yahoo.com.

Payment Details

To complete your child’s enrollment at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, payment must be made according to the selected payment plan (Term Fees or Pay-As-You-Go).

How to Pay

  1. Bank Transfer (Preferred Method): You can make payments directly to our account.
    • Account Name: Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester
    • Account Number: 19215268
    • Sort Code: 30-54-66
    • Reference: [Child’s Full Name]

    Important: Please include your child’s full name as the payment reference to ensure we can allocate your payment correctly.

  2. Cash Payments (By Prior Arrangement Only): Cash payments must be arranged on the day with a member of the SVCSM team.

Payment Deadlines

  • Term Fees: Payments must be made before the first session of each term to secure your child’s place.
  • Pay-As-You-Go: Payment must be made on the day of attendance before the lesson starts.
  • Sibling Discount: Each additional sibling receives a 25% discount on their fees.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Missed Classes: No refunds or credits for missed sessions.
  • Withdrawals: Written notice at least one week before the new term starts is required to withdraw your child.
  • Event-Based Refunds: If an event is cancelled by us, a refund or credit may be issued.
